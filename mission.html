Our Mission

dotLabour seeks to increase understanding of digital technology within the Labour Party by working with elected Labour Party politicians and respective officers to inform and advise on supporting the sector, specific digital technology use cases, and enhancing user rights.

Our beliefs

  • We believe that digital technology will be the most transformative and widely used technology in the world.
  • We believe the UK holds a competitive advantage in the creation and implementation of digital technology and the sector has the potential to dominate the UK economy.
  • We believe the Labour Party should be leading and shaping the growth of digital technology and the sector.
  • We share values which have driven the success of digital technology and will abide by them at all times:
    • transparency
    • user-led design
    • accessibility
